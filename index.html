Why Kaizen?

Kaizen, meaning change for the better, is a Japanese philosophy that embodies our approach to beauty and personal growth.

Experience the essence of kaizen at Kaizen Hair Studio, where Minh's artistry brings innovation and creativity to life with every cut and stroke, fostering personal growth and beauty. Our salon is a sanctuary of transformation, where each visit is an opportunity for reinvention. Join us for a journey of self-transformation and endless possibilities.

Meet Minh

Minh is a premier hair stylist trained at the prestigious Vidal Sassoon Academy in Santa Monica, California.

In addition, Minh has received industry gold standard certifications, including becoming a Goldwell Master Colorist and a Natural Beaded Rows Extension Specialist.

In the salon, Minh shines when working with blondes, mastering dimensional colors through techniques like balayage and color melting. Her expertise allows her to craft the sought-after lived-in look, blending artistry with precision to create effortlessly beautiful hair.
